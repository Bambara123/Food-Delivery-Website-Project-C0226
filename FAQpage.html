Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?
You can easily place an order by browsing our menu, selecting the items you want, and proceeding to checkout. Follow the prompts to complete your order.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and online payment platforms like PayPal and Apple Pay.
How long does delivery take?
Delivery times may vary depending on your location and order volume. Generally, we strive to deliver within 30-45 minutes.
Is contactless delivery available?
Yes, we offer contactless delivery to ensure the safety of our customers and delivery personnel.
Can I modify or cancel my order?
Modifications or cancellations can be made before your order is prepared for delivery. Contact our support team for assistance.
